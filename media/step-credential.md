# Pick your credential

MacroDeploy runs on **your** Claude credential, so the cost is yours, not ours.

- **Claude subscription (Pro/Max)** — no per-token charges. Just be signed in to
  Claude Code (`claude` once, or `claude setup-token`).
- **Anthropic API key** — billed per token; pasted once and stored in your OS
  keychain (never in settings).

Optionally add your **MacroDeploy token** to unlock the full private audit rubric.
Everything is configured through the guided **Set Up / Sign In** command.
